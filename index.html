SpinWin Wheel

SpinWin Wheel packs multiple classic party games into one app: spin custom wheels, race animated ducks, play Truth or Dare, and enjoy drinking-game modes — all designed to keep any gathering fun and unpredictable.

  • Spin the Wheel — Create custom wheels with your own options. Pick winners, assign tasks, or add surprise to any event.
  • Duck Race — Bet on adorable animated ducks in a light-hearted, competitive race.
  • Truth or Dare — The classic social game with a fun twist. Perfect for any group.
  • Drinking Game — Interactive party modes with customizable rules. Play responsibly.
